Earn a competitive salary

At Newton School, we recognise the value of your expertise and knowledge, and we offer a competitive salary package to our mentors. You will be compensated for your time and effort, and we ensure that our mentors are well-rewarded for their contributions to our students' success.

Flexible timings

We understand that your time is valuable, and that's why we offer a flexible mentoring opportunity that allows you to teach and guide our students by investing just a few hours each week, giving you the flexibility to balance your professional and personal commitments.

Easy access to all the resources

As a Newton School mentor, you will have access to our online learning platform, as well as our library of resources and teaching materials. This ensures that you are fully equipped to provide our students with the best possible learning experience.

Opportunity to impact thousands of lives

You will have the opportunity to make a lasting impact on the lives of our students. You will play a critical role in helping them develop their technical skills and pursue their career goals, which can have a significant impact on their lives and the tech industry as a whole.
